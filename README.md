# WG3_6GPlatform_SurveyResults
This repository contains the results of the survey conducted by the Working Group Innovation Management of [6G Platform Germany Project](https://www.6g-platform.com/).

The repository contains:
- The figures generated by the LimeSurvey of the survey results in PNG format.
- The documents generated by the LimeSurvey of the survey results in PDF format.
- The raw data of the survey results in xls format.
